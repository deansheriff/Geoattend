# Employee Guide

## Clock In / Out
- Open the Clock In page on mobile.
- Confirm you are within an allowed geofence (green indicator).
- Tap Clock In or Clock Out.
- Start and end breaks from the status panel.
- Optional photo capture can be enabled by your admin.

## Timesheet
- View daily entries, locations, and total hours.
- Filter by date range.
- Export your attendance CSV.

## Privacy
- Location is captured only during clock in/out.
- GPS coordinates are stored for audit and compliance.
